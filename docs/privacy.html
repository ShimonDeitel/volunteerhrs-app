Privacy Policy

Volunteerhrs is an offline-first app. All of your data - entries you log, notes, and settings - is stored locally on your device using the app's own local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is no analytics tracking and no advertising SDK.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We never see or store your payment information.
